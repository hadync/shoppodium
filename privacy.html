Privacy Policy

Effective July 19, 2026

ShopPodium is a platform where automotive shops keep a public record of their work and take bookings. This page explains what information we collect and what we do with it, in plain language.

What we collect

From shops: your name, email, shop name, phone number, city, the services you offer, your hours, your logo, and the jobs you log (vehicle, service, price, photos, dates).

From customers who book or request a quote: your name, phone number, vehicle, the service you want, and your preferred time.

What we do with it

Shop records (install counts, vehicles, services, photos, and dates) are displayed publicly on the shop's page. That is the point of the platform. Prices a shop collects are private to that shop and are never shown publicly.

Customer information is shared only with the shop the customer contacted, so the shop can do the job and stay in touch about it. We do not sell anyone's information, and we do not share it with third parties for marketing.

Text messages

We send transactional text messages only: a text to a shop when a customer books through their page, and confirmation texts to customers about their appointments. You receive texts only if you provided your phone number in a form on our site. Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts, or HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies with booking activity. Phone numbers collected for texting are never sold or shared for marketing.

Sign in with Google

If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google to create your account. We do not access anything else in your Google account.

Where your data lives

Data is stored with Supabase and served through Vercel, standard, reputable infrastructure providers. We keep your data as long as your account exists. If you want your account and its data deleted, contact us and we will remove it.

Cookies and analytics

We use only what is needed to keep you signed in and to understand basic usage of our own pages. No advertising trackers.

Changes and contact

If this policy changes, the date above changes with it. Questions or deletion requests: message us through your dashboard, or contact the shop that gave you your booking link.
